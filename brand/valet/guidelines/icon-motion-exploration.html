THE ATTENDING STATE

When Valet is thinking · two geometries · three motions each

A personal intelligence at work needs a visible thinking state — the signal that the attendant is present and considering, not absent or stalled. For a classical-register brand, the motion must read as quiet consideration, not consumer-AI theatrics. No sparkle, no pulse-and-glow, no rotating orb. Slow cycles, low amplitude, refined easing.

Three motion candidates are explored for each of the two icon finalists — I·01 a·02 (The Post) and I·02 a·03 (The Binding, thickened center). Animations run live below. Each animates only transform and opacity (GPU-accelerated, no layout thrash). All respect prefers-reduced-motion.

Cycle duration
2.0 – 3.0 seconds. Slower than consumer AI. Considered, not frantic.
Amplitude
Small. Scale ≤ 18%, opacity ≥ 0.4. The form stays recognizable.
Easing
Symmetric cubic-bezier, no bounce, no elastic. Attending, not performing.
Properties
Transform and opacity only. No layout animation. Reduced-motion fallback.
