This document is the complete record of the Valet brand: wordmark, icon, motion, color, type, voice. Every decision is locked. Every asset in this directory was generated from these specifications deterministically.

Icon Mark

The Binding — three horizontal marks of varying width, stacked, with a thickened center bar. The form references the visible bookbinding marks on a well-kept spine: the evidence of pages gathered, bound, and enduring. Register is architectural, not illustrative: no literal books, no pages, no binding tools.

Voice

Discrete. Attentive. Enduring. The register is classical service rendered in contemporary language. No faux-Victorian cliché, no consumer-AI cheer, no builder-economy hype. The prose is direct, quiet, and honest about what the product does.

A sample in voice

What Valet is, in the voice Valet will speak in.

Valet is an edge-resident personal intelligence. It runs on your silicon. It learns you over time, and acts through the tools you already use — not in place of them. Nothing leaves the device.

There is no cloud tenancy to audit, no metering to negotiate, and no session to end. The conversation continues as long as the device is yours. What the cloud calls personalization, Valet calls attendance.

07

Usage

The short version. Full rules live in GUIDELINES.md.

DO

Use the dark (white) wordmark on the dark stack. The canonical form.
Keep clearspace equal to the cap height on all four sides.
Use Cardo for body prose; DM Mono for UI; Bebas Neue for display.
Ship the icon motion with prefers-reduced-motion fallback.
Keep the accent rare — 10% of visual weight by the 60-30-10 rule.
Say what Valet is (local, resident, continuous) rather than what category it is in.

DO NOT

Use amber, blue, pencil, green, or signal red near Valet marks — each belongs to another product or to the house.
Equalize the bar widths in the icon — the narrow-wide-narrow silhouette is structural.
Use Fraunces, Cormorant, or any other reflex serif — Cardo is the voice.
Animate the icon with any motion outside the specified Sweep.
Use consumer-AI visual language: gradient orbs, sparkles, anthropomorphic blobs, spinning loaders.
Use service-worker iconography: bells, keys, keyholes, trays, silver platters.
