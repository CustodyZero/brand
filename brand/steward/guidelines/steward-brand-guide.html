06

Voice

Discrete. Attentive. Enduring. Same register as Valet. The stewardship framing is implicit in the name; avoid leaning into resource-management language that pulls toward platform positioning.

A sample in voice

What Steward is, in the voice Steward will speak in.

Steward is an edge-resident personal intelligence. It runs on your silicon. It learns you over time, and acts through the tools you already use — not in place of them. Nothing leaves the device.

There is no cloud tenancy to audit, no metering to negotiate, and no session to end. The conversation continues as long as the device is yours. What the cloud calls personalization, Steward calls attendance.

07

Usage

Identical DO/DON'T rules to Valet. Full rules in GUIDELINES.md.

DO

Use the dark (white) wordmark on the dark stack. The canonical form.
Keep clearspace equal to the cap height on all four sides.
Use Cardo for body prose; DM Mono for UI; Bebas Neue for display.
Ship the icon motion with prefers-reduced-motion fallback.
Keep the accent rare — 10% of visual weight by the 60-30-10 rule.

DO NOT

Lean into stewardship-of-resources framing (management, allocation, oversight). The product is not a platform.
Use amber, blue, pencil, green, or signal red near Steward marks.
Equalize the bar widths or stroke weights in the icon.
Use Fraunces, Cormorant, or any reflex serif. Cardo is the voice.
Animate the icon with any motion outside the specified Sweep.
