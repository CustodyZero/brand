Design Notes
  • Wordmark follows CustodyZero clipPath technique: two identical text elements clipped at the split boundary
  • Canvas: 470 x 80px (matches CustodyZero house standard)
  • Split point: STATION (298.81px) measured from glyph advance widths via fontTools
  • Font: Bebas Neue @ 83.7px, letter-spacing 0.15em
  • STATION in --white (#F2F2EC)
  • ZERO in Signal Red (#C04848)
  • Icon mark: square perimeter (48px within 64px canvas) with gap at bottom-right corner + center node (r=3)
  • Gap represents active monitoring of the boundary; center dot is the local processing station
