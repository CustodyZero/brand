Getting Started
YOUR STATION. YOUR RULES.
StationZero turns your existing cameras into a unified, private security system. No cloud account. No monthly subscription. No footage leaving your home. Connect any WiFi-enabled camera, point it where you need coverage, and StationZero handles the rest — recording, detection, alerts, and storage — all on hardware you own.
Your footage is stored locally. Detection runs on the edge. Alerts reach you through encrypted channels. Nothing is uploaded, shared, or analyzed by anyone but you.

CONNECT YOUR FIRST CAMERA
Make sure your camera is on the same WiFi network as your StationZero device. Most WiFi cameras broadcast a discovery signal — StationZero will find it automatically. If your camera doesn't appear within 30 seconds, you can add it manually using its IP address.
