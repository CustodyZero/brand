Key decisions:

Proportion: Tall rectangle (G1/G2/G4/G6) reads more as a zero glyph. Square (G5) reads more as an abstract mark. Which do you want?

Weight: Unified stroke (G2) feels more integrated — one system. Mixed weight (G1) creates hierarchy between frame and slash. Heavy (G4) has the strongest presence and best 16px survival.

Slash: Full diagonal (most variants) is more legible. Short slash (G3) is more designed but weaker at small sizes.
